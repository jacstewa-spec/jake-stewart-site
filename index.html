About

Cross-functional leadership that drives measurable outcomes

I've spent the better part of a decade building and leading marketing functions across agency, B2B, and eCommerce environments, not just executing campaigns, but constructing the teams, systems, and strategic frameworks that make growth repeatable and scalable.

My background spans both sides of the table: I understand what it takes to operate a marketing agency at scale and what corporate marketing leadership demands. That dual perspective informs how I build programs, align teams, and communicate across functions, from the executive suite to the creative team.

Whether leading an internal team of 14 or coordinating an extended staff of 50 across vendors and agency partners, I lead with clarity, accountability, and a bias toward measurable outcomes.
