Cross-Functional Strategic Leadership

Marketing doesn't operate in isolation — and neither do I. I build programs and teams designed to work across organizational boundaries: aligning marketing with sales, partnering with product and operations, and communicating up to executive leadership with clarity.

This means establishing shared KPIs, creating reporting infrastructure that everyone can trust, and leading initiatives that pull in stakeholders from multiple functions without losing momentum or accountability.

02

Demand Generation & Pipeline Marketing

I build demand generation programs that connect marketing activity to revenue — not just lead volume. That means designing full-funnel architectures that account for lead quality, sales alignment, attribution, and conversion rate at every stage.

From top-of-funnel awareness through ABM targeting and mid-funnel nurture sequences, I've built programs for both B2B SaaS and eCommerce environments that produce consistent, measurable pipeline contribution.

Lead generation ABM Pipeline attribution Conversion optimization Sales alignment
03

Lifecycle Marketing

Customer acquisition is only the beginning. I design lifecycle marketing programs that extend the customer relationship — from onboarding and activation through retention, expansion, and re-engagement.

My lifecycle approach treats every stage of the customer journey as a distinct marketing opportunity, with tailored messaging, automation sequences, and performance tracking that connects customer behavior to retention metrics and LTV.

Customer journey mapping Retention programs Email automation LTV optimization
04

Cross-Channel Campaign Execution

I've planned and executed integrated campaigns across paid media, SEO, email, social, trade, events, and web — coordinating creative, media, analytics, and sales enablement into a unified program.

This isn't channel management in siloes. It's understanding how channels interact, where the leverage points are, and how to sequence a campaign so each touchpoint reinforces the last — building toward a conversion, not just a click.

Paid media Email SEO Event marketing Trade media Web / CRO
05

SEO / AIO / GEO & LLM Visibility

Search has fundamentally changed. Ranking on Google is now only part of the picture — brands must also be present and credible in AI-generated answers, large language model outputs, and generative search experiences.

I've led organic search strategy for eCommerce and B2B brands, reversing multi-year traffic and revenue declines through technical SEO, content architecture, and on-page optimization. I've also led initiatives specifically designed to address the AI-driven shift in how audiences discover and evaluate brands — including LLM visibility, answer engine optimization (AEO), and generative engine optimization (GEO).

Technical SEO Content strategy AIO / GEO LLM visibility Answer engine optimization

Marketing Technology & Automation

I approach martech as a strategic function, not an IT concern. I've designed and implemented reporting frameworks, CRM-integrated campaign workflows, marketing automation systems, and AI-driven processes that improve team efficiency and campaign scalability.

My goal is always a technology stack that gives leadership real visibility into what's working and why — and gives the team the tools to move faster without sacrificing quality or accuracy.
